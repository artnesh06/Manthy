Manthy is an on-chain NFT survival game built on the Cosmos ecosystem. By using this website and participating in the game, you agree to the following terms. Please read them carefully.

1. Game Overview

Manthy is a browser-based survival game where participants stake their Manthy NFTs, maintain them by feeding daily, and compete to be among the last 50 NFTs standing. Winners receive physical merchandise, exclusive 1/1 digital art, and a permanent Survivor Badge on their profile.

2. Soft Staking

Manthy uses a soft staking mechanism. This means:

  • Your NFT is never locked on-chain. You retain full custody and ownership of your NFT at all times.
  • Staking is recorded in our off-chain database only. No smart contract locks your token.
  • You are free to transfer or sell your NFT at any time. However, if ownership changes, the NFT will be automatically unstaked from the game.
  • Soft staking carries no on-chain risk to your assets.

3. $MTHY Token

$MTHY is a virtual, in-game currency only. It has no monetary value, cannot be traded, withdrawn, transferred, or redeemed for any real-world currency, cryptocurrency, or asset.

$MTHY is earned passively by staking NFTs and is used exclusively to feed your Manthy within the game. It exists solely as a game mechanic to create strategic resource management. We make no representations about any future utility or value of $MTHY beyond its current in-game use.

4. Game Mechanics

  • HP Decay: Each staked NFT loses approximately 1% HP every 30 minutes if not fed. HP ranges from 100% (healthy) to 0% (eliminated).
  • Feeding: Costs $MTHY to restore HP to 100%. Players must feed regularly to keep their NFTs alive.
  • Catching: NFTs with HP ≤ 50% can be caught by other players and moved to the Museum.
  • Auto-Catch: NFTs that reach 0% HP are automatically caught by the system.
  • Museum: Caught NFTs are displayed in the Museum. Original owners continue to earn a small amount of $MTHY.
  • End Game: When 20 or fewer NFTs remain alive, the game ends and survivors are declared winners.

5. Prizes & Rewards

The last 50 surviving NFTs are eligible for prizes:

  • Exclusive 1/1 digital artwork (delivered to a wallet address provided by the winner)
  • Physical merchandise (shipped to an address provided by the winner)

Prizes are awarded per NFT, not per user. If one user has multiple surviving NFTs, they receive multiple prizes.

Shipping costs for physical merchandise are the responsibility of the winner. We are not liable for any shipping delays, customs fees, or delivery issues once the package has been dispatched.
