FAQ
Is my NFT safe?
Yes. This is soft staking — your NFT never leaves your wallet. We only read ownership data from the blockchain. You can sell or transfer your NFT anytime.
How fast does HP drop?
-1% every 30 minutes. From 100% to 0% takes about 50 hours (~2 days). At ≤50% your NFT becomes catchable by other players. At 0% the system auto-catches it.
Can I withdraw $MTHY?
No. $MTHY is an in-game point used only to feed your NFTs. It cannot be traded, transferred, or converted to real currency.
What happens if I sell my staked NFT?
The system checks ownership every 30 minutes. If your NFT is transferred or sold, it gets automatically unstaked and any pending $MTHY is credited to your account.
How are winners decided?
The last 50 NFTs alive in the garden win. Winners are ranked by HP (highest first), then by stake date (earliest first). One wallet can win multiple prizes if they have multiple surviving NFTs.
What do winners get?
Each winning NFT receives a 1/1 art piece, official merchandise, and a permanent Survivor Badge on their profile. Shipping costs are covered by the winner.
My NFT is in the museum. Do I still earn?
Yes. If your NFT was caught, you still earn 16 $MTHY/day as the original owner. This continues for the rest of the game.
